High concentration of fatal overdoses in Overtown

The majority of the 236 people who fatally overdosed in Miami-Dade County with heroin, fentanyl or both in their blood were young white men (regardless of Hispanic origin) from January 2015 through November 2016. The crisis is particularly pronounced in Overtown, a predominantly black neighborhood where over 90 percent of the deaths were white people from outside the community.
